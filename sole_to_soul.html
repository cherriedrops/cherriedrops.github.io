Sole to Soul

2021 / Studio Project / Collaborative

Sole to Soul is a fictional world featuring the Sole to Soul organisation, where the establishment attempts to normalise feet in society in this parallel universe. Through the lens of the designed artefacts created by these organisations, governments and society, we investigate and interpret our vision and satire on modern propaganda, power, technology and human being.

Technology. Feet. Cult.

Inspired by the cult Ho No Hana Sanpogyo, the Sole to Soul organisation is created, attempting to normalise feet, exploring methods and possibilities, and mocking the manipulative approaches in governments, organisations and societies.
It is not about making our flags visible, but to endeavour an idea without people realising or a chance to fight back. You cannot escape.
