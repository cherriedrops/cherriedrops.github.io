Landscape Photography

2022 / Studio Project / Individual

Landscape photography raises up concerns of photoshopped images on social media through the eyes of Canice. It talked about the unrealistic misinterpretations and expectations of images of the public, and the credibility of photographers. The essay is written in the form of a diary, a notebook, a sketchbook, a talk transcript and a proposal.
