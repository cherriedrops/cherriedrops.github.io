Be a Dream Catcher

2019 / Client Project / Competition

Be a Dream Catcher is a series of leaflets, banners and floor stickers designed for the Hong Kong Community College 2019 orientation to welcome freshmen and encourage them to pursue their dreams bravely.
“Be a Dream Catcher” was originally submitted and chosen as the champion of the HKCC 2019 Orientation Theme and Slogan Competition for the following year (2019-2020).
