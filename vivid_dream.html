Vivid Dream

Mixed Media /
210mm x 210mm x 20mm
2020 / Studio Project / Individual

The series expresses the different vivid dreams the artist experienced, including being trapped, experiencing death, escaping, having lucid dreams, false awakening and sleep paralysis. Real wooden dolls are used to describe the calm and expressionless image of the artist, while the objects slowly merge into a painting, symbolising the vague border between dreams and reality, and the challenges and changes in the artist.

Project C: Art Education Programme @ Fotan “Project C” is an art education project that supports Fotan artists to connect art lovers outside the district. Both applicants and artists can opt for their preferred partners to develop a mentoring relationship. After exploring different possibilities of art creation in 10 months, instructors and students will showcase their artworks and share the creative outcome with the public in Fotan Open Studios 2020.
