/My Manifesto

Playful experience is key.

Design is a playful experience for both me and my audience.
Every brief has its unique topic. The research and development of design is and should be a fun process.
A dull, empty shell with visually appealing elements only is pointless.
Graphics. Multimedia. Interactive. Immersive.
All these methods help to provide an enjoyable multi-sensory experience to the public.

Questioning and communication are key.

Design questions the observed surrounding environment, education systems and human lifestyles.
The deliverables then convey these thoughts and values to the world.
Visual communication is essential but this is just the first step.
Hearing. Smell. Taste. Movement.
All these senses help to express my standpoint to others.

Experimental exploration is key.

Design assist others to explore the world.
A variety of concepts intersect and collide with each other.
It provides a method for people to view from a different perspective.

Design helps me to understand the complexity of our society.
I am a designer but not just a mere graphic designer who specializes in simplistic styles.
Every brief is different. From the known to the unknown, any possibilities of methods and material which might suit the context should be experimented on.
Environmental. Social. Political. Economical.
All these aspects provide new unfamiliar knowledge for experimenting and exploration.

Play. Observe. Explore. Design.
