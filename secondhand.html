Second Hand September

2021 / Volunteer Project / Individual

Second Hand September is a series of posters and logos designed for the Greenwich Refugee aid and Community Enterprise emphasising and encouraging shoppers to buy second-hand for 30 days or more.

The logos are designed with the Oxfam 4Rs: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, and Reinvest. The hands not only symbolises the Second “Hand” September but also give a hand to help out the communities and people in need.
