On Surveillance

2021 / Studio Project / Individual

On Surveillance is a research booklet on decoding the negative impact of biometric surveillance on society, and the possibilities in design related to the topic. The essay is an in-depth research on one of the themes raised in the Sole to Soul project.

This booklet is written in the name of “Worldwide Design Collectives”, where the design activists called for an outright ban on the use of sole reading recognition in the fictional world where the Sole to Soul organisation (STS) exists.
