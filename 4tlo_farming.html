4TLO…Farming.

2020 / Studio Project / Collaborative / Lead Graphic Designer

For the Love Of... Farming is a research-based project to discover and appreciate people who are devoted to farming. The 3-minute film is edited from the interview excerpts to spell out their ideally and their commitment to farming. The 120-page publication further explained our discovery throughout the research in detail.

What is farming? Why some are so devoted to it and others hate it? What is love and where is it? Farming is not only about the interest.  It’s about sustainability. It’s about the environment.  It’s about the lives of millions and billions of people. It’s about educating the public on where their food and products came from. Farming itself is a lifestyle.
