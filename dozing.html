Dozing

2022 / Personal Project / Individual

Dozing. The duality of a comfort zone aiming for precision and the anxiousness of destruction. The distorted orientation of the potteries is shaped in the middle of the 3D printing process.

By continuously pushing, pulling and adding paper materials to the potteries, they are softly and slowly melting, dozing off to the unknown.

This project is featured on Weavers Magazine.
